# Ordering HYLO-Forte eye drops

Use this guide when the HYLO-Forte shelf is looking low. Takes about 10 minutes.

## Steps

1. Count how many boxes of HYLO-Forte are left — check the dispensary shelf **and** the drawer in the consult room. [MARK: confirm both storage spots]
2. If there are **6 boxes or fewer**, it's time to order. [MARK: confirm the reorder trigger number]
3. Decide how many to order. The usual order is one carton. [MARK: confirm the usual order quantity and the current price per box]
4. Add up what the order will cost before you place it.

> IF the order total is over $108: check the free-of-charge card folder BEFORE ordering — we may have FOC credits to use first. [MARK: confirm the exact $108 rule and where the free-of-charge card folder lives]

5. Place the order on the supplier's ordering page. [MARK: confirm the supplier portal link (AFT Pharmaceuticals?) and where the login details are kept]

[Open the supplier ordering page](https://www.aftpharm.com)

6. Write the order in the order book: today's date, what you ordered, how many.
7. When the delivery arrives: check it against the invoice, put the stock away, and put the invoice in Karen's tray.

> If anything doesn't add up (wrong quantity, price looks different), don't guess — leave the order and ask Mark.
