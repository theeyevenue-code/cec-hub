# Ordering copy paper from Officeworks

Use this guide when the printer paper is running low. Takes about 5 minutes.

## Steps

1. Check how many unopened reams of paper are left near the printer.
2. If there are **3 reams or fewer**, order more. [MARK: confirm the reorder trigger number]
3. Open the Officeworks website.

[Open Officeworks](https://www.officeworks.com.au)

4. Sign in with the practice account. [MARK: confirm the account email and where the password is kept]
5. Order the usual paper: **A4 white copy paper, 80gsm, one 5-ream carton**. [MARK: confirm the exact brand/spec and usual quantity]

> IF the total is under the free-delivery amount: add something else we're low on (pens, sticky notes) so we don't pay for delivery. [MARK: confirm Officeworks' current free-delivery threshold]

6. Set delivery to: **Concord Eyecare, 161 Concord Road, North Strathfield NSW 2137**.
7. Pay with the practice card. [MARK: confirm which card and where it's kept]
8. When the order confirmation email arrives, forward it to Karen (or print it and put it in her tray).

> Paper usually arrives in 1–2 business days. If it hasn't come after 3 days, check the order status on the Officeworks site before ringing them.
